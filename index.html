How to get involved

Three steps. No gatekeeping. Just shipping.

  1. 01

    Apply to the community

    Fill out a brief application. Tell us what you build, what you've shipped, and what you're looking for. We review weekly.

  2. 02

    Pick up your first bounty

    Browse open tasks — design, development, research, writing. Complete one and earn your first on-chain credential. Contribution history is public.

  3. 03

    Build your on-chain reputation

    Over time, your completed work, earnings, and peer verifications accumulate into a verifiable profile — the currency of the Superteam ecosystem.

Common questions

No. Superteam Ukraine is for builders who are based in Ukraine or have strong ties to the Ukrainian tech ecosystem. Nationality is not a requirement — contribution and quality of work are.

Development (Rust, TypeScript, Anchor), design (product, UI/UX, brand), marketing and growth, research, content, and operations. All skills are valid if there's demonstrable output to show.

Grants are reviewed by a rotating council of senior Superteam Ukraine members. Applications are scored on technical clarity, real-world impact, team credibility, and viability on Solana. Decisions are made within 3 weeks of submission.

Superteam Ukraine is a chapter of Superteam — an official partner of the Solana Foundation. We operate independently but within the broader Superteam framework and maintain direct relationships with the Foundation for grant funding and ecosystem support.

New bounties are posted every Tuesday and Friday. Emergency bounties with tighter timelines can appear on any day. Notifications go to our Telegram channel first, then the official Superteam Earn platform.
