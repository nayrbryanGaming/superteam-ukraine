Our Story

From Kumeka Team
to Superteam Ukraine

We are rebranding from Kumeka Team to Superteam Ukraine — the #1 Solana Web3 hub in Ukraine. Since our founding, we have supported founders, developers, and creators building on Solana.

Now, as Superteam Ukraine, we connect the best Ukrainian builders with the global Superteam network — opening doors to international grants, bounties, and collaboration.

FAQ

Answers to your most common questions

Superteam Ukraine (ex Kumeka Team) is the #1 Solana Web3 hub in Ukraine. We support founders, developers, and creators building in the Solana ecosystem through education, grants, bounties, and community events.

You get access to grants for startup development and seed funding, cash bounties, employment in hub projects, an active builder community, hackathons including our KUMEKATHON, and an acceleration program for top projects.

No. Superteam Ukraine is open to anyone based in Ukraine or with strong ties to the Ukrainian tech ecosystem. Nationality is not a requirement — contribution quality and commitment are what matter.

Superteam Ukraine is a chapter of Superteam Global — a partner of the Solana Foundation. We operate independently but share the global Superteam infrastructure for grants, bounties, and the Earn platform.

Register your project using our project form. Our team reviews submissions weekly and can connect you with grants, mentors, developers, and the broader Superteam network based on your stage and needs.

Yes. You can participate in events, complete bounties on Superteam Earn, and follow our content without full membership. Full membership unlocks grants, direct mentorship, and community access.
